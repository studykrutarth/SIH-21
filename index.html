The Envisioned Solution

A unified, technology-driven system to bring traceability, accountability, and intelligence to track component management.

Implementation of durable, laser-based QR code imprints on all bulk supply items, including clips, pads, liners, and sleepers. This ensures a unique, permanent identity for every single component from its creation.

Software & AI Solution

üì±

A powerful software ecosystem that connects the physical and digital worlds:

  • Mobile Scanning App: Enables field personnel to scan QR codes and instantly retrieve component data.
  • Portal Integration: Seamlessly links scanned data with the UDM (IREPS) and TMS (IRECEPT) portals for unified data management.
  • AI-Powered Analytics: Generates intelligent reports on vendor performance, warranty tracking, inspection history, and failure analysis to support proactive quality monitoring.
